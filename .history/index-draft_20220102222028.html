Artist's Statement:

    My creative motivations are largely influenced by nature and its organic voice; movement within stillness, positive and negative spaces, and nature’s relationship to our bodies. I have always felt a magnetic connection between my own femininity + masculinity and artistic concepts reflected in nature's textures, its softness, rhythm, and its colorways.

    The natural world is my stage where I explore the intertwining connection between isolation, abandonment, and trauma, with the healing and regenerative qualities of the natural world. Remote landscapes reflect my own feelings of isolation and separation, while at the same time grounding me to a force much greater than myself. I feel cradled creatively and spiritually when I maintain a close relationship with the organic, emotional, strength of all natural things, both outside of myself, and within my own expression as an artist and as a woman.
